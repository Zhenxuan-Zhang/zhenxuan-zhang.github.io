I am a Ph.D. student at Imperial College London supervised by Prof. Guang Yang. My research focuses on medical image reconstruction, modality synthesis, and vision-language-based clinical reasoning. I am particularly interested in integrating large language models, implicit neural representations, and diffusion models to develop agentic AI systems for MRI reconstruction and report generation. My recent works explore uncertainty-aware multi-task learning, interpretable radiology reporting, and cross-modal representation learning. I am passionate about advancing intelligent, transparent, and trustworthy AI for medical imaging applications.
